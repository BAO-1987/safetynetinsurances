Glossary of terms

  • Policyholder

    The person or organization who purchases an insurance policy and pays insurance premiums

  • Insurance premium

    The amount of money that the policyholder pays for life insurance. The premium amount depends on the insurance risk, the age and health of the insured, the policy term, and other factors

  • Beneficiary

    The person or organization entitled to receive the insurance benefit in the event of the insured's death or other specified events

  • Insured risk

    The likelihood of an event occurring for which the insurer is obligated to pay an insurance benefit. For life insurance, the insured risk is the death of the insured

  • Insurer

    The company or organization that provides insurance coverage and pays insurance benefits according to the terms of the policy

  • Insurance benefit/payout

    The amount of money paid by the insurer to the beneficiary or policyholder in the event of the insured's death or other specified events

  • Policy term

    The period of time during which the insurance policy is in effect. Depending on the type of policy, the term may be temporary (e.g., 10 or 20 years) or permanent (e.g., lifetime)

  • Sum assured/insurance amount

    The amount of money paid by the insurer in the event of the insured's death or other specified events
