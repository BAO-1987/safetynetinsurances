Types of policies

  • Term life

    • What it is

      Term life insurance provides protection for a specified period of time

    • Benefits

      Flexibility to adjust coverage amount and premiums. Ability to use the insurance policy as an investment tool

    • Policy terms

      Various term lengths and coverage amounts. Option to renew the policy or convert it to another type of insurance without a medical exam

    Learn more

  • Whole life

    • What it is

      Lifetime life insurance that provides lifelong protection and cash value accumulation

    • Benefits

      Guaranteed lifetime protection. Accumulation of cash value that can be utilized in the future

    • Policy terms

      Fixed premiums and guaranteed benefits. Flexible loan and withdrawal options

    Learn more

  • Universal life

    • What it is

      Universal life insurance with flexible terms and investment options

    • Benefits

      Flexibility to adjust coverage amount and premiums. Ability to use the insurance policy as an investment tool

    • Policy terms

      Guaranteed minimum interest rates. Flexibility to change terms and add additional coverages

    Learn more

  • Individualized plans

    • What it is

      Personalized insurance solutions tailored to meet individual needs

    • Benefits

      Adaptation of the policy to specific life circumstances and financial goals. Wide range of specialized policies

    • Policy terms

      Individualized terms and premiums that can be adjusted according to the client's needs. Option to add additional riders for expanded coverage
